# Marshall Fit — Home Gym Equipment Catalog

This catalog documents all equipment available in the gym and the exercises each piece unlocks. It is the source-of-truth reference for building the workout generator's exercise library.

---

## 1. Power Rack System (the hub)

**Marshall Fit power rack** — heavy commercial-style rack, white uprights with laser-cut numbered holes, black hardware. All stations below are built into or mounted on this rack.

| Station / Attachment | What it is | Exercises it unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| **Integrated dual cable system** | Two cable columns, selectorized stacks. **150 lb per side; up to 300 lb when both sides are linked** (e.g., pulldown, seated row). Single-arm/single-side capped at 150 lb. | Lat pulldown, seated/standing cable row, cable fly, face pull, tricep pushdown, cable curl, single-arm work, straight-arm pulldown, cable lateral raise |
| **Multi-grip pull-up bar** | Fixed top bar, neutral + wide + standard grips | Pull-ups, chin-ups, neutral-grip pull-ups, hanging knee/leg raises |
| **Rack-mounted dip station** | Corner-mounted dip attachment | Dips (chest + tricep emphasis), bodyweight knee raises |
| **BTS Smith attachment** | Bulletproof Fitness ("Victor-Tony-Snake") guided Smith rail that runs on the rack uprights | Smith squat, Smith bench, Smith incline press, Smith overhead press, Smith RDL, Smith calf raise, Smith row |
| **Landmine attachment** | Rack-mounted landmine pivot; loads a barbell | T-bar row, landmine press, landmine squat, Meadows row, landmine RDL, landmine goblet squat, rotational/anti-rotation core |
| **Pritchett pad (chest-supported pad)** | Connects to the rack as a chest support | Chest-supported low row, chest-supported landmine/T-bar row |
| **Rogue leg roller / hip-thrust attachment** | Padded roller mounted to rack | Barbell hip thrust (back braced), rear-foot support for Bulgarian split squats, glute/ham accessory work |
| **Spotter arms + J-cups** | Safety + barbell hooks | Enables all barbell pressing/squatting safely |

> **Note:** No jammer arms. The bulky black units on the uprights are the housings for the cable system, not leverage/jammer arms.
